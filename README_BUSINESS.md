## ğŸ¯ What This System Does

This Retrieval-Augmented Generation (RAG) system transforms your document repositories into an intelligent question-answering assistant. Users can ask natural language questions about your documents and receive accurate, cited answers instantly.

### Key Business Capabilities

- **Intelligent Document Search**: Goes beyond keyword matching to understand the meaning and context of questions
- **Source Attribution**: Every answer includes citations linking back to the original documents
- **Multi-Document Intelligence**: Seamlessly works across multiple documents simultaneously
- **Zero Infrastructure Investment**: Runs entirely on free cloud services
